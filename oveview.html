Key Insights

Complaints, Not Questions

6,530 of 7,045 tickets (92.7%) are complaints, only 428 are enquiries. The system is fielding distress, not just questions.

Product Issues Drive 58.2%

Missing items (29.7%) + wrong item (13.2%) + quality (11.8%) make up the majority of complaints.

California: Critical SLA Hotspot

27.98% SLA breach rate (4x) the national average of 6.8%. Average resolution time of 22.1 hours across 243 tickets.

Missing Tagging & Survey Gaps

~50% of survey forms (2,597 of 4,641) are untagged. 2,869 tickets resolved in 2–24 hours show 0% survey response.

October's Hidden Quality Drop

October hit a record 96% SLA compliance, yet CSAT fell to its lowest point at 61%, speed came at the cost of resolution quality.

Strategic Recommendations

P1: Fix Product & Shipping Mistakes

Audit warehouse packing with the team to stop missing/wrong item errors before packages ship.

P2: Force Ticket Tagging

Make tagging mandatory in the system before a ticket can close, to close the classification gap.

P3: Send Extra Help to California

Add agents or redistribute workload to faster regional teams to address the 4x breach rate.

P4: Fix the Survey Bug

IT to investigate the broken trigger failing to send surveys for the 2–24 hour resolution bucket.

P5: Review October's Service Quality

QA to review agent transcripts to ensure tickets are resolved properly, not just closed quickly.
